Welcome to Mery's Site

I would like to share with you all a little about myself. These are the hobbies and activities I like to do!

I'm a Workaholic

I like what I do. There is no doubt about it. I typically work about 6 days a week because I find pleasure in my job and also in keeping my mind busy. Work is something I have to do, so I'm determined in doing my best every day. And knowing so, helps me feel like a successful person.

Listening to Music

It is very important that music is playing around me all day and every day. I find that when music I like is playing I am a happier person. I like alternative, folk, indie, dance, electronica, and of course contemporary classical music.

Reading

I am an avid reader. I just can't be without a book in my hands in my leisure time. I read about 1 1/2 books a week, so watching TV is not much of an option for me. I only read fiction (literature, sci-fi, paranormal, romance, epic fantasy, and manga.)

Riding My Bike

I like to spend as much time outdoors as I can. And whenever possible - summer only - I just love to ride my bike and enjoy the nature and scenery around me. Bike riding gives you a sense of freedom like nothing else.

Walking at the Park

Like I said above, I'm an outdoors kind of person. During the week it is almost impossible for me to spend any time outdoors, but I make up for it on the weekends where I spend hours at the park.

Student at GA

I have very exciting news! For the next 9 weeks I will be a student at General Assembly. I'm taking FEWD, whish me luck!
